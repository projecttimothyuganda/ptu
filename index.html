Project Timothy Uganda

Since 2002, a group of British pastors has been visiting Uganda to train church leaders. The team, known as Project Timothy Uganda and led by Pastor Gerald Tanner, has held Teach the Bible conferences in Masaka, Kampala, Mbale, Kabale, Gulu, Lira. Kasese and Soroti. For these conferences Project Timothy Uganda has partnered with PAG Uganda, the Church of Uganda, Veritas College and others. Since 2017 Project Timothy Uganda has worked together with Pastor Training International. The conferences are open to church leaders and key workers who are teaching or preaching the Word of God. This ministry is founded on the biblical principle in 2 Timothy 2:2:

And the things you have heard me say in the presence of many witnesses entrust to reliable men who will also be qualified to teach others.
